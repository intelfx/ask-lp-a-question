Q:
How many of the  goals set for PulseAudio have been achieved and what else needs to be done?

A:
Note that I am not too involved with PulseAudio anymore, I have passed on maintainership to a new team who do fantastic work.
Back when I started PA my only goal was to write a better sound  server than "EsounD", a very simple, very old sound server from the  Enlightenment camp. I think that goal we managed to reach a long time  ago.
